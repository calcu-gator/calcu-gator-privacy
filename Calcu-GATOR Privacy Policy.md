# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview

GCalcu-GATOR (GATOR Calc) ("the App") is a combat calculator player aid for tabletop gaming. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data Collection

Calcu-GATOR (GATOR Calc) does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information of any kind. The App has no internet connection, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party integrations.

## Data Storage

All data entered in the App (modifier values, mech configurations, theme preferences) exists only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Third Parties

Calcu-GATOR (GATOR Calc) does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

GATOR Calc does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the updated policy will be posted here with a revised date.
